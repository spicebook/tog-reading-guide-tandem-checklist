Recommended Reading Order

The Recommended Reading order places The Assassin’s Blade at the point where it delivers the strongest emotional punch. It also shifts Tower of Dawn to create a smoother transition between Empire of Storms and Kingdom of Ash.

1. Throne of Glass
2. Crown of Midnight
3. Heir of Fire
4. The Assassin's Blade
5. Queen of Shadows
6. Tower of Dawn
7. Empire of Storms
8. Kingdom of Ash

Chronological Reading Order

The Chronological Reading order follows the original publication sequence, placing The Assassin’s Blade first as the prequel and keeping Tower of Dawn after Empire of Storms, even though both books happen at the same time.

1. The Assassin's Blade
2. Throne of Glass
3. Crown of Midnight
4. Heir of Fire
5. Queen of Shadows
6. Empire of Storms
7. Tower of Dawn
8. Kingdom of Ash
