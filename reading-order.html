Recommended Reading Order

The Recommended order places The Assassin’s Blade where it delivers the strongest emotional punch and slightly rearranges Tower of Dawn to create a smoother bridge between Empire of Storms and Kingdom of Ash.

1. Throne of Glass
2. Crown of Midnight
3. Heir of Fire
4. The Assassin's Blade
5. Queen of Shadows
6. Tower of Dawn
7. Empire of Storms
8. Kingdom of Ash

Chronological Reading Order

The Chronological order follows the publication/chronological sequence, placing The Assassin’s Blade first as a prequel and keeping Tower of Dawn after Empire of Storms even though they happen concurrently.

1. The Assassin's Blade
2. Throne of Glass
3. Crown of Midnight
4. Heir of Fire
5. Queen of Shadows
6. Empire of Storms
7. Tower of Dawn
8. Kingdom of Ash
