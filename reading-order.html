The Recommended Reading Order places The Assassin’s Blade at the moment where it hits hardest emotionally, and moves Tower of Dawn to create the smoothest transition from Empire of Storms into Kingdom of Ash.

The Chronological Reading Order follows release order exactly. The Assassin’s Blade appears first as the prequel, and Tower of Dawn remains after Empire of Storms, even though the events overlap.
