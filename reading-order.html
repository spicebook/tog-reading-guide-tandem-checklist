Recommended Reading Order

This order places The Assassin’s Blade at the most emotional moment and moves Tower of Dawn to create a smoother transition into Kingdom of Ash.

1. Throne of Glass
2. Crown of Midnight
3. Heir of Fire
4. The Assassin's Blade
5. Queen of Shadows
6. Tower of Dawn
7. Empire of Storms
8. Kingdom of Ash

Chronological Reading Order

Follows the original timeline: The Assassin’s Blade first, and Tower of Dawn after Empire of Storms.

1. The Assassin's Blade
2. Throne of Glass
3. Crown of Midnight
4. Heir of Fire
5. Queen of Shadows
6. Empire of Storms
7. Tower of Dawn
8. Kingdom of Ash
