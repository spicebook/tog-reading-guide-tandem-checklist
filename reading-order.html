Recommended Reading Order

This order places The Assassin’s Blade at the point of highest emotional impact and shifts Tower of Dawn for a smoother transition between Empire of Storms and Kingdom of Ash.

1. Throne of Glass
2. Crown of Midnight
3. Heir of Fire
4. The Assassin's Blade
5. Queen of Shadows
6. Tower of Dawn
7. Empire of Storms
8. Kingdom of Ash

Chronological Reading Order

This order follows the internal timeline, placing The Assassin’s Blade first and reading Tower of Dawn after Empire of Storms.

1. The Assassin's Blade
2. Throne of Glass
3. Crown of Midnight
4. Heir of Fire
5. Queen of Shadows
6. Empire of Storms
7. Tower of Dawn
8. Kingdom of Ash
